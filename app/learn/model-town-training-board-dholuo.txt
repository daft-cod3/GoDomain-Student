Model Town Training Board

A tighter training board for route reading, sign placement, and parking practice without bulky visual clutter.

Dholuo translation:

Bango mane adwaro kuom kuonjo ka winjo n’uwo piny (road reading), kok susto mondo ok maplani ng’ambo (sign placement), to jogo piny kuom jo-park ng’ambo joma dhi (parking practice) gi dho timo gi chako marac.

Compact training board
Bango manono (bango manono mar ende) kuom adaok.

Read the board like a route map before moving off.
Ebo bango gi kitini mane u-mapa lok ng’ato othuok (route map) ka otira ka ok nyaka kwenda.

Pick up lane flow, sign positions, and junction controls early.
Lowa dhoot kod mare piny kuom lane, ni kok susto, kod hukum mana ebo winjo ng’ato ji amondi ni junction gi but chien.

Use the board to rehearse observation and parking in a lower-risk space.
Tich mar mare kuom bango mondo u-rehearse observation (kuonjo) kod parkir gi ndiri piny ma romo kendo ngo mana nyim.

Feature 01
Controlled junctions

Spot stop, give-way, and merge points early.
Lowa ma ok-pile (stop), m-giwayo (give way), kod ma u-nyalo lut ni butet—lowa ewinji ka ok yie.

Practise priority decisions before live-road sessions.
Yeyi chuma ma u-mak nyaka ko order (priority) ka ok gikenti live-road.

Use turning pockets without cutting the junction.
Yeyi turning pocket kuom tich mamoko gi m-biro kuom junction.

Feature 02
Lane guidance

Read lane arrows before you move over.
Kuo winjo lane arrows ka ok gima (move over).

Position early instead of drifting late.
Biro kuom mane e-winjo kare—to m-otim dala mar kod otamu.

Use edge markings to hold a clean line.
Tiem maler e-piny (edge markings) mondo u-tha ni ayiela lweg.

Feature 03
Crossing points

Tighten scans near zebra and school crossings.
Kore (tighten) kuonjo ka u-jong gi zebra crossing to school crossing.

Reduce speed before bus-stop activity zones.
Ke dho timo/kwergi speed ka u-pinyore loko ma bende ka bayo bus-stop.

Cover the brake where people may step out.
Guna brake ka romo adier ting’ru ma ng’ato angima.

Feature 04
Parking drills

Repeat angle and reverse parking in marked bays.
Yeyi angle parking kod reverse parking kuom marked bays.

Work on slow steering and bay alignment.
Yeyi steering manono (slow) kod kuwe bay ma lwal.

Build a routine before the real test route.
To chiko nyuto (routine) ka ok gima gi route mane gi test.

Road option
One-way 4-lane traffic
4 lanes

Quick read

All four lanes move in the same direction.
Lame yath-one (all four lanes) nyalo to gi pare ma tim.

The focus is steady lane discipline and clean changes.
Winjo mane loyo: lane discipline ma toroko kod chinge ma i-ot i.

Set up early for turns, bays, and pedestrian points.
Biro kare mondo ok nyalo turns, bays, kod pedestrian points.

Lane 1
Forward movement

Use it for early positioning near the kerb.
Yeyi kendo lane 1 mondo ok loch kare ng’ato kod kerb.

Good for bay access and calm entry control.
I-more mondo ok nyalo bay access to jo-kontrol ma min.

Lane 2
Forward movement

Main through lane once the vehicle is settled.
Gi lane 2, nyalo gik nyand chak kuom through lane ka gari ocheng.

Hold steady speed and avoid unnecessary drift.
Dho steady speed to m-giyo drift ma m-need.

Lane 3
Forward movement

Use for measured lane changes only.
Yeyi lane 3 ka nyalo lane change ma uwinji.

Prepare here before the next board feature.
Pinyore chiemo ebar e-ng’ato ka next board feature.

Lane 4
Forward movement

Outer setup lane for wider turn preparation.
Lane 4 ni lane outer mondo u-prepare kuom turn ma mil.

Merge back in smoothly, one lane at a time.
Geni merge kod lweg ma itar—gi lane emiyo emiyo.

Practice list

Use the entry sign, lane arrows, and no-entry control at the far end to keep the flow one-directional.
Guna entry sign, lane arrows, kod no-entry control kuom nyand piny ni flow one direction.

Practise early mirror checks before moving across lanes because late drift is the main mistake on this layout.
Yeyi kuon mirror check ka ok gima—mana mistake ma mawu: drift dala mame.

Prepare for service bays, pedestrian points, and parking access without cutting across two lanes at once.
To prepare kuom service bays, pedestrian points, kod parking access—to m-otwo cut across two lanes amoko.

The safest option is always to move one lane at a time and finish all observations before the lane change starts.
Jonde ma win: nyalo move gi lane emiyo emiyo, to pinyere kuonjo yath kendo lane change etiyo.

Road option
Two-way 3-lane traffic
3 lanes

Quick read

Traffic moves in both directions on this layout.
Gari gi traffic nyalo nyam gi pare oyier (both directions) gi duto.

Respect the opposing lane at all times.
Tich i kodi opposing lane ka nyalo e-gi.

Use the center lane briefly for controlled turning only.
Guna center lane gi tiang’ gima—kuom turning ma controlled.

Forward lane
Forward movement

This is your live lane for steady travel.
Lane mana: forward lane—ni lane ma nyalo u-kenda.

Stay stable before the next junction or crossing.
To stay stable ka u-pinyore chiemo ki junction to crossing.

Center lane
Shared turn flow

Shared space for holding or turning briefly.
Center lane ni piny mana one osum—holding to turning gi gima tiang’.

Never use it as a normal cruising lane.
